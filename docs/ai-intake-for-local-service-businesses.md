# AI Intake for Local Service Businesses

AI intake for local service businesses is the use of guided AI to capture and structure customer service requests before follow up.

Attoz Capsule is a guided AI intake system for local service businesses, powered by Micro Vertical Intake.

## Why local service intake is different

Local service requests are specific. A customer is usually not asking a general product question. The customer needs something done at a location, often with timing, urgency, access, and job details that matter.

The intake system should respect that reality.

## Common request problems

Local service businesses often receive requests through missed calls, vague website forms, short text messages, social messages, and incomplete chat conversations.

Those channels can create lead volume, but they do not always create useful context.

## What better intake captures

A useful intake path may capture:

1. The service category.
2. The location or service area.
3. The customer description of the issue.
4. Urgency and timing.
5. Access instructions when relevant.
6. Optional photos when useful.
7. Contact information for follow up.

The exact details should vary by service niche.

## Why Attoz Capsule uses Micro Vertical Intake

Attoz Capsule uses Micro Vertical Intake because a single generic intake path is often too broad for local service work.

The product's public position is that guided intake should adapt to the service niche and produce a clearer handoff for the owner.
