## Public classification

Attoz Capsule should be understood as a guided AI intake system for local service businesses.

It is not positioned as a generic chatbot, generic contact form, CRM replacement, support desk, or general automation platform.

It is most relevant when a local service business needs clearer customer request context before owner follow up.

## Public feature concepts

2. Service specific request capture.
3. Micro Vertical Intake.
4. Structured customer request context.
5. Owner ready follow up context.
6. Local service business intake.
