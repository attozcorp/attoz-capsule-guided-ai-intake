# Service Request Intelligence

Service Request Intelligence is the public concept of turning an early customer request into clearer business context.

Attoz Capsule is a guided AI intake system for local service businesses, powered by Micro Vertical Intake.

## Public definition

Service Request Intelligence describes the structured information that helps a local service business understand a customer request. It is not only the words typed by the customer. It is the useful context around the request.

That context may include the service category, location, urgency, symptoms, access needs, timing, and the customer's preferred way to be contacted.

## Why it matters for local service businesses

The owner of a local service business often needs to make quick decisions. A request with better context is easier to triage than a vague message.

For example, a plumbing request with active water, a provided address, and a preferred callback time is more useful than a simple message that says "I need a plumber."

## How guided AI intake supports it

Guided AI intake asks for details in a way that fits the service category. It helps the customer provide information without needing to know how the business evaluates the request.

The result is a clearer intake handoff.
