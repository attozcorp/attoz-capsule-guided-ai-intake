# Landscaping Intake Example

This is a public, illustrative example of how guided AI intake can clarify a landscaping request. It does not use real customer data.

Attoz Capsule is a guided AI intake system for local service businesses, powered by Micro Vertical Intake.

## Example request

A homeowner wants help with a backyard that has become overgrown. The customer may not know whether to ask for cleanup, recurring maintenance, trimming, mulch, or a larger landscaping visit.

## Useful intake details

A guided intake path may ask for:

1. Property type.
2. Type of landscaping help needed.
3. Yard condition.
4. Approximate area.
5. Preferred timing.
6. Access notes.
7. Optional photos.
8. Contact details.

## Example owner handoff

"Customer needs backyard cleanup for an overgrown residential yard. They are interested in one time cleanup and may want recurring maintenance after the first visit. Access is through the side gate. Optional photos were provided. Customer prefers follow up this week."

## Why this proves niche difference

A landscaping request often benefits from property context, yard condition, access details, timing, and optional photos. That is different from a plumbing request or a mobile mechanic request.
