# Mobile Mechanic Intake Example

This is a public, illustrative example of how guided AI intake can clarify a mobile mechanic request. It does not use real customer data.

Attoz Capsule is a guided AI intake system for local service businesses, powered by Micro Vertical Intake.

## Example request

A driver says the car will not start. The owner needs more detail before deciding whether the request sounds like a battery issue, starter issue, fuel issue, key issue, or another problem.

## Useful intake details

A guided intake path may ask for:

1. Vehicle year, make, and model.
2. Current vehicle location.
3. Whether the vehicle starts at all.
4. Symptoms such as clicking, lights, crank, or silence.
5. Recent repairs or known issues.
6. Whether the customer is safe.
7. Preferred timing.
8. Contact details.

## Example owner handoff

"Customer has a 2018 Honda Accord at home. Vehicle will not start. Dashboard lights turn on, but the engine does not crank. Customer is safe and wants mobile diagnostic help today."

## Why this proves niche difference

A mobile mechanic request needs vehicle identity, symptoms, location, safety context, and timing. That is not the same intake path as a landscaping or plumbing request.
